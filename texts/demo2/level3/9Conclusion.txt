The Level 3 assessment confirms that Bras, ov’s NbS assets are exposed to moderate climate risk, dominated by chronic heat and drought. Maintenance costs may rise by 10–15% by 2050 under RCP 8.5, but no extreme risks are expected if proactive adaptation is maintained. The findings emphasize integrating financial forecasting, maintenance planning, and ecological monitoring into Brasov’s NbS strategy. This analysis will be updated once higher-resolution monitoring data from WP4 become available.
