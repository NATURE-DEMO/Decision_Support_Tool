The Hazard Index for Bras, ov was computed using the percentage variation of relevant climate indicators with respect to the baseline period, as derived from EURO-CORDEX simulations. Each indicator (temperature, humidity, drought, precipitation, and solar radiation) was evaluated according to its projected change under RCP4.5 and RCP8.5 scenarios for mid- and long-term horizons. The resulting percentage variations were converted into HI scores (0–5 scale) using the classification thresholds defined in Deliverable D2.1.
