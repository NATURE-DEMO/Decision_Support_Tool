To quantify the economic exposure of the NbS assets, indicative CAPEX and OPEX values were estimated following the WP2 methodology. Capital Expenditure (CAPEX) represents the initial investment for the construction of green spaces, vegetation, and infiltration systems, while Operational Expenditure (OPEX) accounts for annual maintenance, irrigation, and vegetation care. Based on comparable European NbS projects and Romanian cost benchmarks, the average CAPEX was estimated at €0.89 million, with annual OPEX of €0.09 million (10% of CAPEX). These values represent typical expenditures for urban green infrastructure of approximately 10,000 m².
