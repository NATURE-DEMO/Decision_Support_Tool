City of Brașov is situated in the centre of Romania, at 625 meters above sea level, and covering an area of 267 km2.   
Located in the curvature of the Carpathian arc in the vicinity of Brasov there are varied landforms. Thus, west of Brasov are the Southern Carpathians with the most important massifs: Făgăraș, Piatra Craiului, Bucegi, Postăvarul, Piatra Mare and Ciucas mountains,   with peak between 1802 and 2543 m high. The landscape around Brasov also contains a region of sub-Carpathian hills, depressions with a relief of plateaus - Țara Bârsei and Țara Făgărașului.
