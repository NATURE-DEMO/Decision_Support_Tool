The climate is temperate continental, characterized by the transition between temperate oceanic and temperate continental climate, wetter and cooler in the mountainous areas, with relatively low precipitation and slightly low temperatures in the lower areas. Omul peak has the lowest average annual temperature (-2.6 degrees C) and the highest average annual precipitation in the country (1,346 mm).
 
The average annual temperature in the county is 8 degrees C. The absolute lowest temperature in the country was recorded on January 25, 1942 in Bod (-38.5 degrees C). Winds do not cross the depressions much, but on the mountain tops they reach up to 25-30 m/s. The westerly winds bring rain, while the northerly and north-easterly winds help to keep the weather fine.
