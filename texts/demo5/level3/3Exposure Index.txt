The Exposure Index (EI) at Level 3 for the Globocica Dam demo site is calculated using the same approach as in Level 2, but with a more detailed economic valuation of the assets exposed to climate hazards. This means that CAPEX, OPEX and annual revenues (ERC, 2024) [37] are assessed in monetary terms for each component of the infrastructure, as outlined. For the hydropower system, the capital expenditure is divided into two components: initial investment costs (replacement value in EUR/kW, according to JRC, 2024 [38]) and capital rehabilitation costs (WBIF EU news, 2023 [39]) required to maintain long-term functionality. Both CAPEX and OPEX figures were validated in consultation with the hydropower operator and stakeholder.
