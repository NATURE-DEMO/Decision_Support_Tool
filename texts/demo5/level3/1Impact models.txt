As in Level 2, the first step in Level 3 risk assessment analysis involves identifying the relevant climate related impacts. For the Globocica Dam demo site, this includes direct physical damage (from slope destabilization due to landslide and rockfall and spillway blockage), operational disruption (due to sedimentation in the spillway chute, as well as landslide and rockfall operation disruptions to the operational road and building), and maintenance cost increases (due to the blockage of the operational building from rockfall induced damage, etc.).
