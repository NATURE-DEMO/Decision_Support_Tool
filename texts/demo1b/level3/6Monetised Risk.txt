For the sixth and final stage of Level 3, which consists of converting risk into monetary terms, and as defined in Deliverable D.2.1, the Monetized Potential Risk (MPR) is calculated. The values were obtained from the monetization curve, differentiated by impact category (CAPEX, OPEX, Revenues). These values express the expected financial loss in million euros (MEUR) under the selected climate scenario (RCP8.5 – 2050).
