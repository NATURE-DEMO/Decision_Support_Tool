Adaptation measures are detailly described in chapters “Potential NbS” and “Specification of the NbS to be demonstrated”. It should be emphasised that for protection of the Gradaščica River water infrastructure (or any other water infrastructure) systematic solutions have to be implemented. Local small-scale (point) solutions can tackle limited area (e.g., one local erosion hotspot). When addressing a hazard along a longer river reach (such as flooding of a wider area) all the implemented solutions affect the downstream situation, and the solutions have to be considered as interconnected and interdependent systems. Therefore, Adaptation measures for each segment are merged, as a system of solutions. Furthermore, solutions in segment S1 affect hazards and assets in segment S2, and S3; and the segment S2 affects segment S3.
