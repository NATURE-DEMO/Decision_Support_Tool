Calculation of the Potential Risk Index (PRI) encompasses Hazard Index, Exposure Index, and Vulnerability Index calculated in the previous steps of the Level 3 risk assessment. PRI are translated to numerical and descriptive categories (1–5) according to threshold values (according to determined thresholds in methodology from D2.1). As Hazard Index varies, depending on the observed climate scenario and observed time horizon, Potential Risk Index (PRI) values also vary. The results for mean value of the climate indicators and the upper confidence limit value of climate indicators were used for calculations of PRI for RCP 4.5 and RCP 8.5 for mid-term period (2041–2070) and for long term period (2071–2100). Same as in Level 2, alternative parameter was also taken under consideration for describing chronic hazards.
